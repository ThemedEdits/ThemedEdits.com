My Story

Crafting visual excellence since 2023

Hammad Ahmed

21 years Pakistan

Hi, I'm Hammad Ahmed, a 21 years old passionate freelancer with a strong foundation in graphic design and front-end development. While I’m skilled in both fields, my true expertise and creative strength lie in graphic design, where I bring bold ideas to life through visually compelling and purpose-driven designs. From branding and social media visuals to promotional materials, I take pride in creating graphics that not only look stunning but also communicate effectively. That said, my front-end development skills are by no means secondary. I have a solid grasp of responsive web design and modern web technologies, allowing me to build clean, functional, and engaging websites that perform well across devices. Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (BSCS), I combine technical know-how with an artistic mindset to deliver professional results that meet diverse client needs. Whether it's designing a striking visual identity or building a seamless user experience on the web, I'm dedicated to exceeding expectations and constantly refining my craft. I'm always eager to collaborate, grow, and turn creative visions into impactful realities.

How It Began

It all started when I began designing for my own purposes. Using apps like PicsArt and PixelLab, I dove into the world of design without any serious intentions. My interest in design intensified, and soon it became a creative outlet I couldn't ignore. Around the same time, I got into gaming of FIFA Mobile. I also started my own YouTube channel, where I began designing my own thumbnails. I built strong connections with FIFA Mobile YouTubers and became a concept designer for the game, further refining my design expertise and expanding my network. However, after some time, I decided to step away from it all and take a break. But in 2023, I decided to take the leap and pursue it part-time. Started learning Illustrtator and Photoshop and here I am. Fueled by a desire to create for others, I shifted my focus to client work and began hunting for opportunities.

  • Designed over 600+ graphics since launch
  • Served clients from multiple countries
  • Built long-term partnerships through quality and trust

Where We Are Now

Today, Themed Edits has evolved from a solo passion project into a thriving creative endeavor. With a growing portfolio and an expanding client base, I've been able to collaborate with clients from diverse industries, delivering high-quality graphic design and branding solutions. Since officially starting in 2023, I've completed over 200 projects, ranging from logo designs and social media visuals to large-scale branding campaigns. I take pride in the strong relationships I've built along the way, focusing on quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction. While I’m still a one-person team, my ambition and commitment are unmatched. As I continue to grow and refine my craft, I’m excited to take Themed Edits to new heights and tackle even more challenging projects in the creative industry.
